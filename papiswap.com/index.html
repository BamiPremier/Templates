What is $PAPI?

Papillon ($PAPI) is a community-focused, decentralized cryptocurrency with instant rewards for holders.

While existing community-based tokens offer just a trip to the moon, our team is up to build a secure, useful, and easy-to-use Scanner to help you with all of your token analysis and provide you with the main dashboard you'll use when trading all tokens (not just ours).

Keep all your dogs in our PapiPound Scanner, while we work on our roadmap of features. At the end, our aim is to integrate our own swap which will be detailed in our white paper (coming soon).
